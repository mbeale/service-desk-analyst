# ITSM Market Pain Points (2025)

## Market Satisfaction Overview
In 2025, approximately 40% of ITSM users remain dissatisfied with their tooling solutions. The primary sentiment is that complexity often outweighs efficiency gains.

## Key Drivers of Dissatisfaction
- **Support Quality (Top Complaint: 20-30%):** Users cite slow responses and lack of accountability as major issues.
- **Usability & Flexibility:** Usability issues (16%) and limited customization (16%) are cited as major pain points, driven by steep learning curves and rigid workflows. These specific issues account for 19% of product churn.
- **Cost (14%):** High costs and hidden fees, particularly affecting the SMB segment, contribute to poor perceived value.

## Human Impact
- **IT Well-being:** Tool frustration is reported to negatively impact the well-being of 74% of IT professionals.

## Emerging Trends & Risks
- **AI Reality Gap:** [Inference] The "hype" surrounding AI capabilities is currently outpacing actual implementation success, contributing to user dissatisfaction.
- **Security:** Emerging risks (5-10%) are linked to uncontrolled AI adoption and "Shadow Vibe Apps".

## Vendor Specific Data: SolarWinds
*Context: Market perception vs. specific feedback.*
- **Rating:** Holds a strong 4.6/5 Capterra rating for mid-sized teams.
- **Reported Gaps:** Despite the high rating, users consistently report UI clunkiness, limitations in basic reporting, and support delays (e.g., tickets remaining open for >1 month).
