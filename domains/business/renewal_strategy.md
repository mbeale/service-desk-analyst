
# Renewal Strategy

## 2026 Strategic Initiatives

### Pricing & Uplift
- **Base Renewal Increase**: The target range for renewal price increases has been raised to **12-15%** (previously 7.5%).
- **Tier Migration (Upsell)**: A core focus is upgrading customers from the **Advanced** tier to the **Premier** tier.
  - **Projected Value**: This specific upgrade path is projected to yield a **30% revenue uplift**.

### Context
- **Source**: Brooke (February 12, 2026) outlined these focus areas for the renewal motion.
