- [Fact] SolarWinds Service Desk (SWSD) supports HIPAA and HITECH regulations and signs Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with customers and partners.
- [Fact] SWSD possesses SOC 2, ISO 27001, TRUSTe, and Skyhigh Enterprise-Ready certifications.
- [Fact] Security measures include data encryption in transit, restricted physical/logical access, penetration testing, and audit trails.
- [Assumption] SWSD is not intended for storage of medical records; PHI should only be entered incidentally as necessary for service requests.

- [Fact] A signed BAA is required prior to deployment for customers needing HIPAA compliance.
- [Fact] No official government certification exists for HIPAA compliance.
