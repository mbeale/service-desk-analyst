# EasyVista Competitor Profile

## Overview
EasyVista is a service management platform known for deep ITIL compliance and high flexibility for complex workflows. It maintains a strong market position in Europe and is often targeted at organizations with legacy ITIL environments requiring deep customization.

## Pricing & Licensing (2025 Analysis)
### Subscription Costs
- **Estimated User Pricing:** $10 - $20 per user/month (Assumption).

### Associated Costs (Fact)
- **Implementation:** $1,000 - $10,000+ depending on scope.
- **Customization:** $500 - $5,000.
- **Training:** Approximately $500 per user.

## Key Capabilities (2025)
- **EV Pulse AI:** AI-driven features for service management.
- **EV Reach:** Remote support capabilities.
- **EV Self Help:** Self-service portal functionality.

## Competitive Analysis
### Strengths
- **ITIL Compliance:** deeply aligned with ITIL standards.
- **Workflow Flexibility:** High adaptability for complex, non-standard business processes.

### Weaknesses (Inferred)
- **User Interface:** Perceived as outdated compared to modern SaaS leaders, despite recent improvements.
- **Reporting:** Considered slow or less intuitive.
- **Integrations:** Limitations in breadth or ease of integration compared to market leaders.

### Comparison vs. SolarWinds Service Desk (SWSD)
- **SWSD Advantage:** Superior value in ease of use (UI/UX) and speed of implementation/setup.
- **EasyVista Niche:** Preferred for scenarios requiring high-complexity customizations or rigid adherence to legacy ITIL frameworks where modern usability is secondary.
