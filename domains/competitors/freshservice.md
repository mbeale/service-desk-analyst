# Freshservice

## Pricing Model (2025)
- **Starter**: $19/agent/month
- **Growth**: $49/agent/month
- **Pro**: $89-$99/agent/month (Adds Project Management)
- **Enterprise**: $109-$119/agent/month (Adds Freddy AI)
- **Freemium**: Not available.

## Strengths & Weaknesses
- **Strengths**: 
  - Low entry price point.
  - Polished initial User Interface (UI).
  - Fast reporting capabilities.
- **Weaknesses/Gating**:
  - AI features (Freddy) restricted to Enterprise tier.
  - Change Management is gated behind higher tiers.
  - Asset Management limited (gated for >100 assets).

## Competitive Comparison vs. SWSD
- **SWSD Wins**: Superior value for unlimited end-users and deeper integrated ITAM capabilities.
- **Freshservice Wins**: Faster reporting and more modern initial UI experience.
