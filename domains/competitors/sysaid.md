# Competitor: SysAid

## Profile
- **Vertical Focus:** Specializes heavily in the **Healthcare** sector.
- **Key Offerings:** Includes "Automate Joe" for service orchestration and "Copilot" for AI features.
- **Market Perception:** Strong reputation for customer support.

## Pricing Analysis
- **Model:** Hidden/Custom pricing (Quote-based).
- **Estimated Costs (Assumption):**
  - **Help Desk:** ~$79/user/month
  - **ITSM:** ~$108+/user/month
- **Hidden Costs:**
  - One-time onboarding fees.
  - **Add-ons:** "Copilot" AI, Analytics, Remote Control, Sandbox environments, and additional asset blocks.

## Competitive Analysis

### Strengths
- **Support:** High customer satisfaction with support services.
- **Orchestration:** "Automate Joe" provides robust workflow automation capabilities.
- **Niche Fit:** tailored workflows for healthcare providers.

### Weaknesses
- **UX/UI:** The Administrative interface is outdated compared to modern standards.
- **Usability:** High complexity barrier for small teams or low-maturity IT organizations.
- **Cost Structure:** Reliance on paid add-ons reduces predictable TCO.

## Comparison vs SWSD

| Feature | SysAid | SolarWinds Service Desk (SWSD) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **UI Modernity** | Weak (Outdated Admin UI) | **Strong** (Modern, intuitive design) |
| **Pricing** | Hidden, add-on heavy | **Transparent**, predictable tiers |
| **Asset Management** | Costs extra for assets | **Built-in** ITAM & Discovery |
| **Support** | **Strong** | Standard |
| **Target Audience** | Healthcare / Complex IT | General Mid-Market / MSP |
