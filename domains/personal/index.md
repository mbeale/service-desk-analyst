# Personal Domain

## Scope
This domain covers personal knowledge including active projects, interests and learning, professional profile (skills, experience, goals), and a decision journal for tracking major decisions and their outcomes.

## Subdomains
- [Projects](projects/index.md): Active and completed projects with goals, milestones, and progress tracking.
- [Interests](interests/index.md): Topics of interest, learning resources, and insights.
- [Profile](profile/index.md): Skills inventory, professional experience, and personal/career goals.
- [Decisions](decisions/index.md): Decision journal tracking major decisions with context, rationale, and reflections.

## Open Questions
- What are my highest-priority goals for this year?
- Which skills should I prioritize developing?
- What patterns exist across my projects and interests?
