- [Fact] Revenue Funnels are categorized as New Sales, Expansion Sales, Renewals, and Other.
- [Fact] AOV (Average Order Value) is total revenue divided by the number of contracts/deals.
- [Fact] MYPA (Multi-Year Paid Annually) refers to customer contracts greater than 12 month terms paid annually.
- [Fact] GDR (Gross Dollar Retention) measures recurring revenue retained from the existing customer base, excluding upsells/expansion (max 100%).
- [Fact] NDR (Net Dollar Retention) measures recurring revenue retained from the existing customer base, including renewals, expansion, and price increases.
- [Fact] GPIM (Gross Price Increase Multiple) is the average price increase for customers who renewed.
- [Fact] NPIM (Net Price Increase Multiple) is the average price increase across all customers, accounting for churn.
