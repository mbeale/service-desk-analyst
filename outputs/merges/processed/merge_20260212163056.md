```markdown
## Metric Definitions
- **Revenue Funnels**: Categorized as New Sales, Expansion Sales, Renewals, and Other.
- **AOV (Average Order Value)**: Total revenue divided by the number of contracts/deals.
- **MYPA (Multi-Year Paid Annually)**: Customer contracts greater than 12-month terms paid annually.
- **GDR (Gross Dollar Retention)**: Recurring revenue retained from the existing customer base, excluding upsells/expansion (max 100%).
- **NDR (Net Dollar Retention)**: Recurring revenue retained from the existing customer base, including renewals, expansion, and price increases.
- **GPIM (Gross Price Increase Multiple)**: The average price increase for customers who renewed.
- **NPIM (Net Price Increase Multiple)**: The average price increase across all customers, accounting for churn.
```
