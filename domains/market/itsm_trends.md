# ITSM Market Trends (2026)

## Overview
Analysis of key shifts driving the ITSM landscape, focusing on AI adoption, experience-centric delivery, and operational efficiency.

## Key Trends
- **AI & GenAI Adoption:** The market is shifting towards pervasive AI/GenAI for proactive problem anticipation, contextual analysis, and process optimization.
- **Experience-Driven Delivery:** Service delivery is pivoting from "quick resolution" metrics to outcome-focused models emphasizing Employee Experience (EX) and Customer Experience (CX).
- **Governance & Security:** Critical focus on AI governance frameworks and Zero Trust security models.
- **Hyper-automation:** Increasing trend to streamline repetitive tasks via RPA integrations and native automation.
- **Enterprise Service Management (ESM):** ITSM capabilities are actively expanding into non-IT functions (HR, Finance, Legal).
- **Agile & DevOps Convergence:** Deepening integrations to break down silos between Development and Operations.
- **Sustainability (Green IT):** Growing demand for features that track energy usage and asset lifecycle sustainability.
- **Low-Code/No-Code:** High adoption of tools empowering non-technical users to build custom workflows.
