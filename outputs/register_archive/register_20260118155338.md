
### [2026-01-18] Note
**Source:** URL: https://documentation.solarwinds.com/en/success_center/swsd/content/completeguidetoswsd/msps-overview.htm
**Tags:** #ServiceDesk #MSP #Architecture #Licensing #Limitations
- [Fact] Service Desk for MSPs creates a separate, fully licensed tenant for each client attached to the MSP account.
- [Fact] Advanced plan customers can create up to 15 clients; Premier plan customers can create an unlimited number.
- [Fact] Client accounts are isolated tenants; there is no direct access or data leakage between them.
- [Fact] MSP Administrators inherit Administrator role in all client accounts.
- [Fact] MSP Agents are added to client accounts with no defined role to prevent unauthorized access.
- [Fact] Groups and Queues created in the MSP account sync to all client accounts and cannot be modified at the client level.
- [Fact] Deleting a Group or Queue in the MSP account deletes it from all client accounts.
- [Fact] The MSP account provides a unified (federated) view of incidents and service requests from all client accounts.
- [Fact] Enterprise Service Management (ESM) is not currently supported on client accounts.
- [Fact] The Service Desk mobile app is not currently available to MSP clients.
- [Fact] Existing Service Desk ITSM accounts cannot be attached to an MSP account.
