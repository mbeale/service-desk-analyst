- [Fact] Service providers represent distinct departments (e.g., HR, Facilities) within Enterprise Service Management (ESM) accounts.
- [Fact] Only Organization level administrators can create, enable, or disable service providers.
- [Fact] Creation templates include "Blank" (default settings only), "Sample" (generic incidents/catalog items), and specific "HR" or "Facilities" templates.
- [Fact] Service provider URL subdomain prefixes are limited to 10 alphanumeric characters (A-Z, 0-9).
- [Fact] Existing service providers can be duplicated to copy configurations and reduce setup time.
- [Fact] Tickets can be forwarded between service providers if the feature is enabled.
- [Fact] Subscription plans may enforce a limit on the number of active (enabled) service providers.
